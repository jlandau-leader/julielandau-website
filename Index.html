The Shift

You don't become someone new.
You become who you actually are.

Presence that precedes you

You stop performing leadership. You become it. Your team feels the difference before you say a word. The room changes when you walk in. Not because of your title. Because of who you are.

Decisions from your own ground

Not from pressure. Not from fear. Not from what others expect. The right call becomes obvious instead of effortful. You stop second-guessing the person making the decision.

Emotion as intelligence

What you were taught to manage becomes the sharpest thing in the room. You stop suppressing it. You use it. That's not soft. That's the most sophisticated leadership skill there is.

Power without proving

Authority that no longer needs validation. You stop performing competence for rooms that already respect you. You lead as yourself. And discover it was always enough.

About Me

Hi, I am Julie Landau. French born, global citizen.

I've sat where you're sitting.

Not metaphorically. I led teams across Europe and Asia for seventeen years. France, China, Hong Kong, Singapore. Across fifteen countries. Business development, digital transformation, executive leadership. VP at Alibaba. I built from scratch, scaled under pressure, and made the transition from corporate to building something entirely my own.

I've been the athlete who burned out at 27. The executive who kept delivering while something essential went offline. The one who found ways to cope, to keep going, to stay in motion. Quietly avoiding herself. And chose, eventually, to stop. To go all the way in instead.

When I left corporate I didn't stop learning. I trained with some of the most rigorous coaches and teachers in the world. Steve Chandler, Steve Hardison, Ankush Jain, Byron Katie, Michael Neill. I became an ICF PCC within a year. Not because I needed the credential. Because I wanted to go all the way in.

I keep going all the way in. Every year. That's not a selling point. It's who I am.

I didn't read about transformation. I lived it.

What I know is this: the ceiling you're hitting isn't outside you. It never was.

I work with a small number of leaders at any given time who are done performing and ready to live the life they have built for themselves.

If something in this is landing. That's enough to start.

Questions

What leaders ask
before reaching out.

Therapy works with the past. Consulting tells you what to do. This is neither.

We work with who you are right now. The identity running your decisions, your presence, your leadership. Not what happened to you. Not what strategy you should execute. Who you're being while you do all of it.

Most of my clients have done therapy. Some have worked with consultants for years. They come here because neither touched what they're carrying at the level it needs to be touched.

Because performing well is exactly the problem.

You've built a career on delivering. The question isn't whether you can keep doing it. The question is what it's costing you. And whether the version of you that gets to lead at the top for the next decade is the same one who got you here. Or someone freer, clearer, and more fully themselves.

Programs give everyone the same map. This starts with the fact that you are not everyone.

Group coaching is valuable. But there is a version of you that will never show up in a group. The one that's exhausted. The one that's been performing for so long it's forgotten what it feels like not to. That version needs a room with one person in it who isn't going anywhere.

Frameworks tell you how to think. This asks what's underneath how you've been thinking. And who you'd be without it.

You've probably done the programs. You're here because something they couldn't reach is still there.

You don't need to know. Ready is a concept that keeps high performers waiting indefinitely.

What matters is simpler: something in you is paying attention right now. That attention is the signal. The conversation will tell us both whether this is the right fit and the right time.

Every engagement is built around you. Not a template.

We meet regularly in sessions that are unhurried and unscripted. You bring what's alive. I bring full presence, direct honesty, and the ability to see what you can't see from inside it yet.

Between sessions you have direct access to me. I create curated resources, tools, and reflections specific to where you are. Including AI-supported materials that deepen the work between our conversations.

One thing stays constant: your transformation belongs to you. I don't do it for you. I create the conditions where it becomes inevitable. You do the seeing. I make sure nothing stays hidden.
